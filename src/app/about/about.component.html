NUTRINOVEL
This all started around a large, rectangular, white table in the software egineering room in the CSE Department at UNT. Four students — Lupe Rivera, Stephen Ho, James Arnold, and AnneMarie Sabatini — gathered around, feasted on mango monster energy drinks as we contemplated upon what we should do to contribute to our society. Being college students, colloquially poor people, we understood the struggle of trying to eat healthy and also cheaply. Thus, NutriNovel was born: a quick and easy way to access various nutrition information about fast food.
